Questions people ask

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

GEO is making your business the source an AI assistant names and quotes when it answers a question. Where SEO aims for a page-one ranking, GEO aims to be cited by name inside answers from ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Google's AI Overviews.

How is GEO different from SEO?

SEO earns a ranked link on a search results page. GEO earns a mention inside a synthesized AI answer. They share the same foundations — real-intent content, structured data, and authority — but GEO adds machine-facing signals like an llms.txt brief and explicit AI-crawler access.

Does GEO require paid advertising?

No. COLAClear earned its Google AI Overview citation and page-one rankings with zero paid media — entirely through the eight mechanics in the playbook.

Does this only work for beverage alcohol?

No. Beverage alcohol is where it was proven, but the eight mechanics apply to any regulated, high-consideration category where buyers research before they commit.
